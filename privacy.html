中文版

映见提词由三千映见提供。我们重视你的隐私。本隐私政策说明映见提词 V1 如何处理信息。

1. 我们收集的信息

映见提词 V1 不要求注册账号，不收集姓名、手机号、邮箱、通讯录、精确位置、照片、麦克风录音或摄像头画面。

应用中的文稿、标题、显示设置、语言偏好等内容默认保存在你的设备本地。我们不会把这些内容上传到服务器。

2. 本地储存

为了提供文稿库、自动保存、语言切换、字号、速度、镜像、阅读线等功能，应用会在设备本地保存必要数据。这些数据仅用于应用自身运行。

如果你删除应用、清除浏览器网站数据或重置设备，本地保存的数据可能会被删除。

3. 文件导入与导出

当你导入 TXT 文稿时，应用只会读取你主动选择的文件内容，用于生成本地文稿。导出文稿时，应用会把当前文稿保存为文本文件，导出的文件由你自行管理。

4. 蓝牙键盘和遥控器

映见提词支持蓝牙键盘或翻页器控制播放、暂停、调速和翻页。应用只响应设备系统传入的按键事件，不识别、不记录、不上传蓝牙设备身份信息。

5. 第三方服务

映见提词 V1 不包含第三方广告 SDK，不进行跨应用追踪，也不使用第三方分析服务收集你的使用行为。

6. 儿童隐私

映见提词面向一般提词与口播创作场景，不主动面向儿童收集个人信息。如果监护人认为儿童在使用过程中输入了不应保存的内容，可以在设备本地删除相关文稿或删除应用。

7. 数据安全

由于文稿保存在你的设备本地，请妥善保管设备、系统账号和备份。我们建议你为重要文稿自行备份导出的 TXT 文件。

8. 隐私政策更新

如果后续版本加入云同步、账号、摄像头、自定义服务或其他需要处理更多数据的功能，我们会更新本隐私政策，并在适当位置提示你。

9. 联系我们

如果你对隐私政策或数据处理方式有疑问，请发送邮件至 sjszl@vip.qq.com。

English

Yingjian Prompt is provided by Sanqian Yingjian. We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Yingjian Prompt V1 handles information.

1. Information We Collect

Yingjian Prompt V1 does not require account registration and does not collect your name, phone number, email address, contacts, precise location, photos, microphone recordings, or camera footage.

Your scripts, titles, display settings, and language preference are stored locally on your device by default. We do not upload this content to our servers.

2. Local Storage

To provide the script library, autosave, language switching, font size, speed, mirroring, reading line, and related features, the app stores necessary data locally on your device. This data is used only for the app's own functionality.

If you delete the app, clear browser website data, or reset your device, locally stored data may be deleted.

3. File Import and Export

When you import a TXT script, the app reads only the file you choose and uses it to create a local script. When you export a script, the app saves the current script as a text file that you manage yourself.

4. Bluetooth Keyboards and Remotes

Yingjian Prompt supports Bluetooth keyboards and page-turning remotes for play, pause, speed adjustment, and navigation. The app only responds to key events provided by the operating system. It does not identify, record, or upload Bluetooth device identity information.

5. Third-Party Services

Yingjian Prompt V1 does not include third-party advertising SDKs, does not perform cross-app tracking, and does not use third-party analytics services to collect your usage behavior.

6. Children's Privacy

Yingjian Prompt is intended for general teleprompter and speaking scenarios. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If a guardian believes a child has entered content that should not be stored, the related local script or the app can be deleted from the device.

7. Data Security

Because scripts are stored locally on your device, please protect your device, system account, and backups. We recommend exporting important scripts as TXT files for your own backup.

8. Updates to This Policy

If future versions add cloud sync, accounts, camera features, custom services, or other features that require additional data processing, we will update this Privacy Policy and provide appropriate notice.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how data is handled, please contact us at sjszl@vip.qq.com.
